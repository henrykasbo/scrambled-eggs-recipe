How to Cook Your Eggs Scrambled

There are so many ways to cook your eggs but in this recipe I will show you one of the most basic methods: How to scramble your eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Slice of Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Ham (optional)
  • Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Before heating a pan, crack eggs in a bowl and whisk them with a fork
  2. When the pan is heated at medium heat, add butter and spread evenly around pan
  3. Add whisked eggs in, then gently and repeatedly fold the edges of the egg over itself towards the center
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add ham and cheese to your liking
  6. When egg is no longer runny, take off the heat and enjoy!
